Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HostingAura ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our speed test service at speed.hostingaura.com.

1. Information We Collect

For all users (no account required):

  • IP address
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Geographic location (country/region)
  • Speed test results (download speed, upload speed, ping)
  • Timestamp of the test

For registered users:

  • Email address or phone number (used for account creation and OTP verification)
  • Encrypted password (we never store plain-text passwords)
  • All speed test results linked to your account
  • Account creation date

2. OTP Verification & Communications

When you register for an account, we send a one-time password (OTP) to verify your identity. This is sent via:

  • Email — sent from noreply@hostingaura.com
  • SMS — sent from HostingAura to the phone number you provide
📨 OTP messages are sent through ClickSend, our third-party communication provider. Your email or phone number is transmitted to ClickSend solely for the purpose of delivering the verification code. ClickSend does not use your data for marketing or any other purpose. Learn more at clicksend.com/privacy-policy.

OTP codes expire within 10 minutes and are permanently invalidated after use.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide accurate speed test results
  • Generate shareable result links
  • Store your test history when you have an account
  • Verify your identity during registration via OTP
  • Improve our service quality and analyze network performance trends

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in a secure database hosted on our servers. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text. OTP verification records are stored temporarily and marked as used immediately after verification.

Guest test results are stored with a unique test ID but are not linked to any personal identity. Registered user results are linked to your account and accessible only when logged in.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • ClickSend — for OTP delivery via email and SMS
  • Cloudflare — for CDN, DDoS protection, and IP/location detection
  • ipapi.co / ip-api.com — for ISP detection during the speed test

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties beyond what is necessary to operate the service.

6. Data Retention

  • Guest test results — retained indefinitely for service improvement; not linked to personal identity
  • Account data — retained until you request deletion
  • OTP records — automatically expire after 10 minutes

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account
  • Opt-out of data collection entirely by using the service as a guest (no account)

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your last 5 speed test results locally on your device for guest users. This data never leaves your browser and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser storage. We do not use tracking cookies.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, data access requests, or account deletion, please contact us at: privacy@hostingaura.com
